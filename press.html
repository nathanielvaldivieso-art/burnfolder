story

photonegative began as a question about retakes — whether every new version deplatforms an older self that deserved the last word. building the album led to burnfolder: an artist-built archive where the release cut is frozen and the process stays visible in the darkroom. music made slowly, released on purpose — on a site that doesn't treat you like inventory.
