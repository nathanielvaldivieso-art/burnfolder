photonegative is in an interesting place.

all 4 songs have seen 10+ versions now. I'm about 6 months into actively pursuing this concept. a lot of the work feels really good, i notice myself get better constantly, not just at the music but at focus, organization and other life skills that i never pictured myself being good at.

in creating photonegative, i also began to conceptualize burnfolder. i began to really consider what leverage in art looks like. i gained faith in myself that i didnt realize i lacked. i bought a camera, became interested in design, and began to holistclly aim towards the story i wanted to tell. realizing that this concept needs to live in multiple meduims to make sense.

this work is far from done, but the music I'm making right now is making me really happy. if i were to never work on photonegative again, id listen back to it and be proud of what i created.

although i feel satisfied, i also know that everytime i work on it the project gets better. it improves slowly but it improves nontheless. sometimes it will take a full mix to realize the vocals are wrong and that i need to retake. it took months of practice on the drums before i realised that i could play more expressively on "it doesnt matter". I worked for weeks to perfect the sound. Now, a few months of practice later, and i feel like i can redo and make the drums even better (and im probably right).

after the dust settles from all the retakes, the song is usually better. i am also probably a better musician after all of the work. yet, i listen back to the older versions of the song and i think "damn.. it was great then too". its interesting becuase if i listen to the project as it was 6 months ago, the songs are completely different in every way (lyrics, instrumentation and structure). Currently, everything about the records sounds better to me. Yet, i wonder if i wait another year would i feel the same way about the versions i have now?

so if i continue to edit my this music, am i deplatforming a version of myself that deserves the last word?

when do i take the picture?

when do i begin to chip away at authenticity by working towards it?

here is the project im talking about

PHOTO NEGATIVE
